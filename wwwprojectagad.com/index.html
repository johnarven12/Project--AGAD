Project: Actuation of Geographic Landslide Data (AGAD)


Promoting safety to landslide-prone areas through intelligent risk assessment and prediction.


Info and area of Interest

We are using previous landslide reports from NASA to feed our linear regression model.Through the use of a regression model and NASA’s historical data, we can assess and predict the possibility and severity of a landslide in an area.n addition, there are unrecorded incidents(at nasa database) throughout our history, thus relevant social media posts are found useful in providing timely and relative data.|Our primary area of interest is in the Philippines.

Landslide Report Map(History)

These are previously reported cases stored in NASA data storage.It is important to take record of previous landslide locations because some landslide reoccur within the same coordinates.

Landslide Report Map(Predicted)

The locations loaded in this map are from the regression analysis taken from possible factors that might trigger a new landslide case.

Landslide Report Map(Searches)

The locations loaded in this map are from social media,news,publications, and google searches that might add to the record of landslide data or add into analysis of factors that triggers landslide.

Features

Near realtime webscraping data collection

Linear Multivariate machine learning analysis

Linear fitting machine learning that increases accuracy as the data increases.

Notification to subscribe individuals

NASA data Landslide histories

Data are collected from previous NASA reports.

Importance of data sources(webscraping and satellite data)

Web scraping is essential to the process because it allows for the quick and efficient extraction of data in the form of news from various sources.
Such data can then be processed to gain the necessary insights. Satellite data for landslides can be used in a variety of ways, including disaster risk reduction through mapping and analysis of landslide-prone areas, and disaster response by mapping events shortly after they occur.
